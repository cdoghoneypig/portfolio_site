Nextdoor Events

Beginning with a challenge to create a subsystem for a special category of events on Nextdoor's platform, we learned quickly through research that the event system on the network was failing. Users longed for human contact with their neighbors, but were dimly aware that Nextdoor offered local events, with few having attended any and no one interviewed willing to host.

We refocused our efforts on quick-wins for events throughout the iOS app, designing a system for personal invitations, feed sorting by public guestlists, and threaded comments on event pages.

We delivered a fully animated Flinto prototype of the event screens of the app and further recommendations, based on research, for how Nextdoor could sponsor local events to increase engagement on the network.

  • Role: Research & Design
  • Client: School project under Susan Wolfe
  • Tools: Typeform survey, interviews, Sketch wireframes, Flinto prototype, a hundred post-it notes
  • Duration: 3 weeks
  • Year: 2016
